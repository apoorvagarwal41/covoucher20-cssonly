We help local hotels through the pandemic

CoVoucher20 is a platform for consumers to plan holidays and help local hotels through the pandemic. Anyone can start planning their holidays now to escape the confinement, and we mutualize the business risk and ensure that a hotel will be able to accommodate your stay.

How it works

  1. local hotels create an account, enter their location, and provide the details of their business.

  2. Businesses share a direct link to their CoVoucher20 page with their customer base.

  3. Consumers sign up and buy vouchers that they would like to use after the pandemic.

  4. Businesses receive money immediately and are better able to cope.
